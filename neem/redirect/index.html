Overview

One such tree is the Neem tree (Azadirachta indica), widely known for its numerous benefits, including its significance in Ayurveda.

Benefits of Neem:

  1. Medicinal Properties: Neem leaves, bark, and seeds are used in Ayurveda for their antibacterial, antifungal, antiviral, and anti-inflammatory properties. It is used in treating skin diseases, fevers, and dental issues.
  2. Skin Health: Neem paste and oil are commonly used to treat acne, eczema, and other skin disorders. It helps in detoxifying the blood and promoting clear, healthy skin.
  3. Pest Control: Neem oil is an effective natural pesticide and insect repellent, making it beneficial for agriculture.
  4. Environmental Benefits: Neem trees are excellent for improving air quality, as they absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen.
